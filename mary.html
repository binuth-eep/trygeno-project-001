Who is Mother Mary?

Mother Mary, also known as the Virgin Mary, is one of the most beloved figures in Christianity. She is honored as the mother of Jesus Christ and a symbol of purity, humility, and faith. Her life inspires millions around the world to live with love, compassion, and trust in God.

Life of Mary

Annunciation

The angel Gabriel appeared to Mary and told her she would give birth to Jesus.

Birth of Jesus

Mary gave birth to Jesus in Bethlehem in a humble manger.

At the Cross

Mary stood by Jesus during his crucifixion, showing ultimate strength and faith.

Inspirational Quotes

"Behold the handmaid of the Lord; be it unto me according to thy word."
"My soul magnifies the Lord, and my spirit rejoices in God my Savior."

Prayer to Mother Mary

Hail Mary, full of grace, the Lord is with thee. Blessed art thou among women, and blessed is the fruit of thy womb, Jesus.
